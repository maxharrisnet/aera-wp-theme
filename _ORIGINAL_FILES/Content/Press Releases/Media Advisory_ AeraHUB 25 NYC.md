**Aera Technology Showcases the Enterprise Impact of Agentic Decision Intelligence**  
**at AeraHUB 25 NYC** 

***Reveals New Research; Highlights Outcomes from The Hershey Company, Gallo, AstraZeneca, and WGU; and Unveils the Next Wave of Agentic AI Innovation***

**Mountain View, Calif. — October 21, 2025 —** [Aera Technology](https://www.aeratechnology.com/), the [decision intelligence](https://www.aeratechnology.com/what-is-decision-intelligence) leader, today announced [AeraHUB 25 NYC](https://www.aeratechnology.com/aerahub-2025), its annual global summit taking place on November 4, 2025, on site at the Times Center in New York City and virtually to a worldwide audience. The flagship event will bring together executives from leading brands for a day of insights, customer stories, and live demonstrations that will showcase the impact of agentic decision intelligence on enterprise operations and performance.

Decision intelligence has reached a critical turning point — moving beyond pilots to enterprise-scale deployment. By delivering fast, measurable outcomes, it is redefining how organizations operate, compete, and prepare for the future.

This transformation will take center stage at [AeraHUB 25 NYC](https://www.aeratechnology.com/aerahub-2025). where industry innovators and visionaries will share how digital decision-making is reshaping enterprise performance and the future of work.  From freeing working capital and reducing inventory risk to improving service levels, responsiveness, and sustainability, decision-makers at every level are unlocking value. 

Attendees will also gain an exclusive first look at new global research on decision intelligence adoption and scaling, and experience a live demonstration of how [Aera](https://www.aeratechnology.com/) — the first decision intelligence agent — builds, deploys, and scales agentic skills that learn, adapt, and act in real time.

Aera Technology pioneered the field of [decision intelligence](https://www.aeratechnology.com/what-is-decision-intelligence) with the launch of [Aera](https://www.aeratechnology.com/) and the software platform that powers it, [Aera Decision Cloud™](https://www.aeratechnology.com/aera-decision-cloud). The company continues to lead this product category, helping global enterprises achieve fully connected, intelligent operations that drive efficiency, resilience, and growth.
